About Us

Our Vaccine and International Travel Center staff are specialists in vaccinology, immunization procedures, infectious diseases, infection prevention and control, public and global health. Broad-scoped expertise and attention to detail sets us apart from all others. Access to language lines as well as staff who speak multiple languages are value-added services.

Pre-travel consultation

  • Vaccines and preventive medication
  • Prophylactic medication
  • Personal safety during travel
  • Management of chronic medical conditions during travel

Location

We are located at 550 S. Jackson Street in downtown Louisville on the second floor of the University of Louisville Ambulatory Care Building in the 550 clinic. For specific directions, click here for Google assistance.

Parking is conveniently located in the parking garage that is connected to the Ambulatory Care Building. Handicap parking is available by the elevators on levels one and two of the garage. A walkway on the second level of the garage takes you directly to the entrance of the Ambulatory Care Building. Turn right, then follow the signs to the 550 Clinic Vaccine and International Health and Travel Center. If you prefer on-street parking, there are meters located on Chestnut and Floyd Streets that offer up to four hours for parking.
